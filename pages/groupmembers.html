About Us

We are a group of researchers who are passionate about understanding the properties of low dimensional materials. Our research focuses on exploring the electronic, optical, and mechanical properties of materials such as graphene, nanotubes, and 2D materials.

Our group uses a variety of techniques, including scanning probe microscopy, Raman spectroscopy, and first-principles calculations, to investigate the properties of these materials. We collaborate with other research groups around the world to further our understanding of low dimensional materials.
